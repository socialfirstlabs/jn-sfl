Empowering Nepali Dreams

We are J&N Caregiver Training Co. Ltd., dedicated to equipping Nepali individuals with the skills and confidence to build successful caregiving careers in Japan.

Our Mission

Bridging Cultures Through Care

Our mission is to create a seamless pathway for compassionate Nepali individuals to enter the Japanese healthcare system. We believe that with the right training and cultural understanding, our graduates can provide exceptional care to Japan's elderly population while securing a prosperous future for themselves and their families.
